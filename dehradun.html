Dehradun

The Gateway to the Himalayas

Surrounded by the majestic Himalayan foothills, Dehradun is a beautiful hill city known for its pleasant climate, lush greenery, and a mix of adventure and spirituality. The city serves as the capital of Uttarakhand and is a perfect getaway for nature lovers, trekkers, and peace seekers.
Some of Dehradun’s must-visit attractions include:

  • Robber’s Cave, a unique natural cave where a river mysteriously disappears and reappears.
  • Sahastradhara, a breathtaking waterfall and sulfur spring known for its medicinal properties.
  • Tapkeshwar Temple, an ancient cave temple dedicated to Lord Shiva, where water drips naturally on the Shiva Linga.
Dehradun is also home to prestigious institutions like the Forest Research Institute (FRI) and Doon School, adding an intellectual charm to the city. Its strategic location makes it the ideal base for exploring nearby destinations like Mussoorie (The Queen of Hills), Rishikesh (The Yoga Capital), and Haridwar (The Gateway to the Gods).
For adventure lovers, Dehradun offers thrilling activities like paragliding, trekking, and river rafting. The local markets, like Paltan Bazaar, are great for shopping handicrafts, woolens, and the famous Dehradun Basmati rice. Whether you’re looking for peace, adventure, or a mix of both, Dehradun is the perfect destination.
