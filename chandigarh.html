Chandigarh

Chandigarh, India’s first planned city, is a masterpiece of urban design and architecture. Designed by the legendary Le Corbusier, it features wide, tree-lined avenues, well-organized sectors, and a perfect balance between nature and modern infrastructure. Serving as the capital of both Punjab and Haryana, the city is known for its cleanliness and high standard of living.
The Rock Garden, a unique attraction made from industrial waste, showcases creativity at its best, while Sukhna Lake provides a tranquil escape for nature lovers. The Zakir Hussain Rose Garden, Asia’s largest, adds to the city’s charm with thousands of blooming flowers. Chandigarh is also an education hub, home to Panjab University and several prestigious institutions.
For food lovers, the city offers a taste of authentic Punjabi cuisine, from rich butter chicken to refreshing lassi. Shopping at Sector 17 Plaza or the upscale Elante Mall is a treat for visitors. Despite its modern appeal, Chandigarh retains a relaxed atmosphere, making it one of the most livable cities in India. Whether you're a nature enthusiast, a history buff, or a foodie, Chandigarh’s charm is sure to leave a lasting impression.
