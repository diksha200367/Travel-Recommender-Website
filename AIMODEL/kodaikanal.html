Kodaikanal

The Princess of Hill Stations

Tucked away in the Western Ghats of Tamil Nadu, Kodaikanal is a picturesque hill station that captivates with its rolling hills, dense forests, and pristine lakes. Famous for its cool climate and scenic landscapes, it is often referred to as the Princess of Hill Stations. At the heart of Kodaikanal lies the serene Kodaikanal Lake, perfect for boating and relaxing by the shore. The nearby Coaker’s Walk offers panoramic views of the surrounding valleys and distant hills, making it ideal for a quiet walk amidst nature. Nature lovers will enjoy the beauty of Pillar Rocks, Bear Shola Falls, and Bryant Park, while Liril Falls and Devil’s Kitchen add a touch of adventure. A unique aspect of Kodaikanal is its abundant flora, including the rare kurunji flowers that bloom once every twelve years. The town is also known for its homemade chocolates, tea plantations, and eucalyptus trees, adding to its charm. Whether you’re looking for a peaceful retreat or a place to indulge in nature walks, Kodaikanal offers an unforgettable experience.
