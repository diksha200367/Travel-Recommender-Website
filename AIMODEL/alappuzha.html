Alappuzha

Venice of the East

Alappuzha is a stunning backwater paradise in Kerala. It is famous for its houseboat cruises along the tranquil Vembanad Lake, offering breathtaking views of lush paddy fields and coconut groves. The Alappuzha Beach, with its historic pier, is perfect for a peaceful evening walk and witnessing mesmerizing sunsets. The city is also renowned for the Nehru Trophy Boat Race, held annually, showcasing Kerala's rich tradition of snake boat racing. Visitors can explore the Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, which is home to numerous migratory birds, making it a paradise for nature lovers. The traditional Ayurvedic spas in Alappuzha offer rejuvenating treatments, making it an ideal wellness destination. With its serene charm, vibrant culture, and delicious seafood, Alappuzha is an idyllic getaway that provides a perfect blend of relaxation and adventure.
