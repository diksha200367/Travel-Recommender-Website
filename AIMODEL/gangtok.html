Gangtok

Gateway to Sikkim's Beauty

Perched atop the Himalayas, Gangtok, the capital of Sikkim, is a charming town that offers awe-inspiring views of the surrounding mountains and valleys. Known for its cool climate, beautiful landscapes, and vibrant culture, Gangtok is the gateway to the natural beauty of Sikkim. The town offers breathtaking views of Kangchenjunga, the third-highest peak in the world, and is a favorite spot for trekking and mountain biking. One of the major attractions is the Rumtek Monastery, a center for Tibetan Buddhism that exudes serenity. The Tsongmo Lake, situated at an altitude of 12,300 feet, is another must-visit, especially for nature lovers and photographers. Visitors can take a trip to Nathu La Pass, a high-altitude trade route connecting India with China. Gangtok’s MG Road is a bustling street lined with shops, cafes, and restaurants, making it the perfect spot for a leisurely stroll. The town’s mix of traditional Buddhist culture and modern amenities make it an ideal destination for tourists. Whether exploring monasteries, enjoying panoramic views, or savoring the local cuisine, Gangtok is a slice of paradise in the northeastern Himalayas.
