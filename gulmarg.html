Gulmarg

A city in Kashmir

Gulmarg, located in the Baramulla district of Jammu and Kashmir, is a breathtaking hill station known for its pristine beauty and adventure activities. Nestled in the Pir Panjal range of the Himalayas, Gulmarg is famous for its snow-covered landscapes, lush green meadows, and the worldâ€™s second-highest operating cable car, the Gulmarg Gondola. During winter, it transforms into a premier skiing destination, attracting adventure enthusiasts from across the globe. In summer, the meadows bloom with vibrant flowers, making it a perfect retreat for nature lovers. Gulmarg is also home to the Gulmarg Golf Course, one of the highest in the world, and the serene Alpather Lake, which remains frozen for most of the year. With its mesmerizing views, adventure sports, and tranquil ambiance, Gulmarg rightfully earns its title as the "Meadow of Flowers" and remains a must-visit destination in Kashmir.
