Bhubaneswar

Temple City of India

Bhubaneswar is renowned for its ancient temples, including the Lingaraj Temple and Mukteshwar Temple, showcasing exquisite Kalinga architecture. The city is also a gateway to the Buddhist sites of Dhauli and Udayagiri, offering spiritual and historical insights. Bhubaneswar's rapid modernization is evident in its IT hubs and smart city initiatives, making it a city of contrasts where history and progress coexist. Visitors can explore Odisha’s art and culture at the Odisha State Museum and Ekamra Haat, where traditional handlooms and handicrafts are on display. The local cuisine, featuring delicacies such as Dalma and Pakhala Bhata, provides a delightful culinary experience. Bhubaneswar’s well-planned infrastructure, cultural richness, and historical significance make it an essential stop for travelers seeking to explore Odisha’s diverse heritage.
